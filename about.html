Who am i?

I'm Douwe and i'm currently 19 years old. As said before, i'm a student at De Hogeschool van Amsterdam and i'm currently studying Communication and Multimedia Design.

What do i like to do?

In my free time i mostly like to play games, draw and have fun with friends. Thats also why i like working in groups because its more fun, and i like meeting new people. I also participate in Tennis and like to visit second hand stores.

What are my interests?

I'm mostly interested in Graphig Design and Programming, so thats the reason i plan of focusing on those subjects in my second year. I'm also interested in working with tiny computers like Arduino's and RasberryPI, and building contraptions with them.

Where do i see myself in the future?

In the future i will probably be working for a big company which specialises in either graphic design or programming. At the same time i wouldn't be suprised if i have my own little group that i make websites with. I would love to work in both the design and programming side at the same time.
